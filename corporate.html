Design elements

Color justification

  • #24a200 - Reform stands for self-growth and the brand logo contains a plant in this green color. Bright green stands for renewal and rebirth based off the new growth and revitalization of plant life.
  • #201c1c - Using this colour shade, we wanted to create a modest and mature colour scheme. Our brand is about learning from mistakes and being self-reflective which translates to being mature and modest.
  • #ffffff - The color white relates to space for making mistakes, which we give to our customers. It is also associated with self-acceptance which is one of our brand values.

Font justification

As our main font, we chose Montserrat because this font has very clean lines and its geometric design gives it a contemporary feel, making it suitable for our target audience as it correlates to something 'new'. Additionally, this font is available in different weights and styles, allowing for flexibility in usage and design. Furthermore, we chose Montserrat because it has a great readability due to its well-proportioned letters (medium.com, 2023).

User interface pattern

  • Rows: used to distribute the content evenly across the page.
  • Sections: used to distribute our content across the page (e.g. header, main, and footer).
  • Contact us button: used to easily give the customer access to our contact details.
  • Learn more button: used to direct the customer towards the corresponding page.
  • Product gallery: used to transparently showcase the products we have to offer without as little clicks as possible.
  • Footer: used to always have our office's location, opening hours, and contact details on every single page.
  • Hero banner: used to showcase the title of the page.
  • Instagram embedding: used to quickly show the latest posts of our Instagram page.
  • Dividers: used to give a sleek design and indicate the start of a new section.
  • Headers: used to clearly indicate the start and title of a section.

Testing report

Testing goals

  • Find out if the home page is clear
  • Find out if purchase process is clear
  • Find out if contacting process is clear
  • Find out if chat bot feature is needed
  • Find out if feedback page is clear
  • Find out if social media access is clear

Testing methods

Participant information

  • 5 participants
  • All participants use a car
  • Average age of the participants is 23
  • All participants are located in the Netherlands
  • All participants look have interest in a car
  • All participants first search for a car online
  • All participants travel to work or school
  • All participants also participated in the solution and problem interviews

Setting information

  • Prototype was made using paper and by hand
  • All testings were done at team member's house
  • Participants gave permission to being recorded

Test conduction information

  • Testing was recorded by interviewer (team member)
  • Participants were asked to introduce themselves first
  • Participants were asked to rate the home page, make a purchase, find a way to contact our brand, find one of the social media pages button, find customer review page, and find information about the brand

Testing results

  1. Overall impression of the website was positive for everyone
  2. Home page was clear
  3. Social media access was clear
  4. Purchase process was clear
  5. Contacting process was clear
  6. Chat bot feature was not clear for everyone
  7. Feedback page was not clear for everyone
  8. Participants would go for feedback to social media pages
  9. Finding information about brand was clear
  10. Website was not properly structured

Improvements to the final website

  • Chat bot feature removed since other contact options were clear for everyone (contact button)
  • The website was made more structured by the use of design patterns
  • The feedback page was removed since it caused more confusion than positive responses. Additionally, the participants stated that they would visit the social media page for feedback from customers

MARKETING

Campaign context

This campaign was aimed at introducing and strengthening our brand its presence on social media platforms, in particular Instagram, TikTok and Facebook. The main idea was to consider mistakes as opportunities for growth and learning, which is in line with the spirit of our brand - self-reflection and self-improvement. Various promotional events were conducted to attract our target audience, create a community, and increase brand loyalty.

Objectives

Specific: Facebook Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook Reach 2,000 people by April 15th with a strategic social media campaign. This target is based on a study conducted by Statista, according to which the average coverage of accounts with fewer than 10,000 subscribers is 30% (Statista, 2023).

Measurable: Measure success using key performance indicators (KPIs) such as reach, engagement (likes, comments, posts), subscriber growth, and website traffic through social media.

Achievable: The conclusions obtained as a result of comprehensive marketing research and initial consumer surveys indicate that the established goal is true, given the noted high level of engagement of the target audience on these platforms.

Relevant: This goal fits well into a broader marketing framework aimed at increasing brand awareness and stimulating community growth that reflects the core values that underpin it.

Time-bound: The goal should be by April 15th.

Target Audience

Our target audience consists of Dutch residents who are active on social media, particularly Instagram, TikTok and Facebook. Based on research and interviews, we have developed a detailed understanding of their demographics, behaviors and preferences:

  • Age: 23 years old (average)
  • Gender: 60% male, 40% female
  • Hobbies: Socializing, creative and outdoor activities
  • Social media activity: Most active in the evening any day of the week
  • Occupation: Predominantly in retail, sales or students

  • Mode of transportation: Car

Learning Points

What we have learned

Platform-specific strategies: Each social media platform requires a different approach to optimize engagement. For example, TikTok's short, creative videos attracted a lot of attention, while Instagram posts and stories were more effective for detailed content and direct interaction.

Audience engagement timing: Posting posts in the evenings significantly increased interactions, which validated our decision to align content scheduling with audience engagement patterns.

What worked

Visual Consistency: Consistent use of brand colors, logos and hashtags across all platforms drove brand awareness and trust.

Interactive content: Surveys, Q&A sessions and user-generated content effectively engaged audiences and provided valuable feedback for future campaigns.

What did not work

Limited use of the platform: Initial focus primarily on Instagram limited our overall reach. Engaging TikTok and Facebook from the beginning could have expanded our audience more effectively.

Short campaign duration: A two-week campaign was not enough to gain significant momentum and reach our target reach. A longer campaign duration would have given more time for audience growth and engagement.

What worked out as expected

Instagram engagement: High engagement rates with posts and stories confirmed our choice of Instagram as our primary platform.

Community Building: Successfully built a sense of community around our brand values, validated by user-generated content and repeated interactions.

What did not meet expectations

Overall reach: Failure to reach the 2,000 goal indicated the need for a broader, multi-platform strategy and more aggressive promotional tactics.

Engagement metrics: While engagement rates were high, converting them into tangible subscriber growth was a challenge.

What would be done differently

Increase use of platforms: Use a multi-platform approach from the start to maximize reach and take advantage of each platform's unique strengths. Increase campaign length: Increase campaign length to allow more time for audience engagement and growth.

Publish more frequently: Implement a more regular publishing schedule across all platforms to maintain consistent engagement and audience retention.


By reflecting on these points, we gained valuable insights to optimize future campaigns to better achieve our marketing goals and build a stronger community around the Reform brand.

Reflection on the campaign

The marketing campaign its core message centered around self-reflection and self-acceptance, embodied in the slogan "Celebrate your mistakes. " The campaign included posts, stories, and reels on Instagram, which were strategically scheduled after 17:00, as well as with the peak activity of our audience. Every post was written to stick to the brand values and included our logo and relevant hashtags to increase its visibility. Nevertheless, the campaign did not achieve the goal of getting 2000 people by April 15th, as the reach was only 75. This discrepancy highlighted several areas for improvement:

  • Platform limitation: This restriction to only using Instagram was our main weakness. Our polls also showed that the other platforms our target demographic used were TikTok and Facebook.
  • Content variety: Although posts and stories received engagement, reels were less explored and could have engaged more with dynamic content.
  • Duration: There needed to be more than two weeks of campaign period to create the much-needed buzz and audience participation.

Future planning

Building on the insights from our initial campaign, several strategies can enhance future marketing efforts:

  • Expand social media platforms:  Consider using TikTok since it is popular among the target demographic and Facebook. Studies also show that the target population widely accesses these platforms. Combining these platforms would mean using one platform to promote the other; it is possible that this would double the traffic (Scorpion, 2023). TikTok its approach to short informative videos and Facebook its wider audience reach could expand and enhance the campaign its reach. In addition to expanding the platforms, added visuals should also be put into consideration. For instance, the use of animations to bring the brand its message to life, lifestyle photography to showcase other people embracing themselves, and infographics to pass the brand its message in a visually appealing manner.
  • Enhance content strategy: More posts can increase the engagement level, and the diversification of the posts presented can also help. It is possible to add various forms of content like product information, brand values, customers' stories, quizzes, questions, and answers to maintain the audience's interest. For instance, sharing behind-the-scenes content exhibiting the processes involved in running the Reform brand would likely give the audience a more personal connection to the brand and its values. It is also essential to post on certain days of the week; for instance, the weekend, when people have more time to spend online, can also help create a posting routine.
  • Utilize analytics and feedback: Monitoring the engagement level and collecting the audience's feedback regularly is essential. Using Instagram Insights, one can get information regarding the performance of the post, the audience, and the engagement rate. It should inform the changes in content and the alterations in the strategies used. For instance, if posts about brand values elicit more engagement, more such content should be made.
  • Longer campaign duration: It is also important not to set the campaign for less than two weeks because it can limit the target audience's potential exposure. This way, the audience will be retained for a long time, and there will be constant interaction, hence the continuous engagement. Periodically analyzing the campaign and making modifications based on the results will assist in meeting the outlined objectives (Scorpion, 2023).
  • Incorporate user-generated content: It is recommended to actively invite users to share their materials related to the brand on social networks, as this will increase the organic coverage rate. User-generated content provides a genuine recommendation, which can help create a feeling of togetherness among the brand's followers. This effect can be further enhanced by running contests or challenges where users produce content with the help of brand hashtags.
  • Refine target audience engagement Further segmenting the target population can help understand them better and, in turn, customize the campaign (Zotara, 2023). Communicating with various target groups through specific content and interactions can improve the bond with the brand. For instance, reaching out to particular posts to a segment that has specific interests or behaviors will make the campaign more relevant to that segment.

Detailed process improvements

Reflecting on the process, several adjustments can enhance the campaign's effectiveness based on our experience:

  • Content planning and creation: The content should be designed to be appealing and professionally done. Beautiful images and videos that align with the brand's image can attract more viewers' attention and deliver our message.
  • Monitoring and analytics: Use social media analysis features to track interactions and optimize the approaches used in social networks. It is thus essential to assess the performance frequently to discover what strategies are more effective and adapt the campaign accordingly.
  • Community building: The emphasis should be on creating a community based around the brand. By responding to followers' comments and direct messages and participating in community activities, the customer base can be built, and people will promote the brand.
  • Learning from mistakes: On this basis, one should learn from one's mistakes and work toward the campaign's main principle. Recording a campaign's progress or lack of progress can be helpful in future endeavors.

Conclusion

These were the key messages of the first series of Reform's marketing campaigns: the achievements and the challenges. The subsequent campaigns can also increase engagement by using more platforms, improving the content, prolonging the campaign, and using targeted ads and interactive features. Implementing these recommendations and considering the process will ensure that our marketing communicates the brand's message and values and builds a supportive community.
