Our Brand

We are a team of students who have built a brand to change the world.

We got the task to create our own brand and together with a motivated team we brought this task to success. We focused on what the customers demand are and discovered a gap in the market and build Reform.

The team consists of 2 members that are all goal-driven and focused on creating more than just a brand. To represent and stand for making mistakes and learn from them instead of seeing them as a weakness. Together we can make a change in the car industry and build a respected community.

Our Values

Strength

We believe that people's biggest power is letting themselves make mistake and learn from it.

Self Acceptance

By giving customers space for mistakes, we give them the opportunity to accept themselves.

Self Reflection

We believe that reflecting on ourself leads to an understanding of why we made the mistake and how to prevent it in the future.

Self Growth

We focus on the importance of learning from mistakes and growing into a stronger version of yourself.
