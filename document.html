Justification Document

Campaign context

This campaign was aimed at introducing and strengthening our brand its presence on social media platforms, in particular Instagram, TikTok and Facebook. The main idea was to consider mistakes as opportunities for growth and learning, which is in line with the spirit of our brand - self-reflection and self-improvement. Various promotional events were conducted to attract our target audience, create a community, and increase brand loyalty.

Objectives

Specific: Facebook Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook Reach 2,000 people by April 15th with a strategic social media campaign. This target is based on a study conducted by Statista, according to which the average coverage of accounts with fewer than 10,000 subscribers is 30% (see Appendix A).

Measurable: Measure success using key performance indicators (KPIs) such as reach, engagement (likes, comments, posts), subscriber growth, and website traffic through social media.

Achievable: The conclusions obtained as a result of comprehensive marketing research and initial consumer surveys indicate that the established goal is true, given the noted high level of engagement of the target audience on these platforms.

Relevant: This goal fits well into a broader marketing framework aimed at increasing brand awareness and stimulating community growth that reflects the core values that underpin it.

Time-bound: The goal should be by April 15th.

Executed Promotional Activites

1. Creating and publishing content

Instagram 

  • Posted a mix of static images, carousels, stories and clips.
  • Content topics included brand values, product features, customer testimonials and behind-the-scenes footage.

TikTok

  • Created engaging short videos about trends and issues related to the automotive industry and our brand's unique message.

Facebook

  • Reposted Instagram posts, utilized Facebook Stories, and created event pages for promotions and special announcements.

2. Interactive Engagement

  • Conducting polls and Q&A sessions on Instagram Stories and Facebook.
  • Organized a contest on Tik Tok that invited users to share their “Reform Moments” by telling stories of personal growth.
  • A referral program was launched where users could share posts, earning discounts and incentives.

3. Consistency of visuals and messaging

  • Uniform colors, logos, and hashtags were used across all platforms to increase brand awareness.
  • Posts focused on themes of self-development, acceptance, and unique features of Reform vehicles.

4. Community Building Activities

  • Engaged with subscribers through comments, direct messages, and user-generated content.
  • Created a series of Reform Stories highlighting customer experiences and successes.

Target Audience

Our target audience consists of Dutch residents who are active on social media, particularly Instagram, TikTok and Facebook. Based on research and interviews, we have developed a detailed understanding of their demographics, behaviors and preferences:

  • Age: 23 years old (average)
  • Gender: 60% male, 40% female
  • Hobbies: Socializing, creative and outdoor activities
  • Social media activity: Most active in the evening any day of the week
  • Occupation: Predominantly in retail, sales or students
  • Place of residence: Netherlands
  • Mode of transportation: Car
